CSE 282: An Introduction to Bioinformatics Algorithms (Winter 2014)

Instructor: Dr. Pavel Pevzner  |  Teaching Assistants: Kai Zhang & Olga Botvinnik  |  Lectures: 3:30-4:50 Mon/Wed, in CSE (EBU3B) 2154

Prerequisites

The course assumes some prior background in biology, some algorithmic culture (an equivalent of CSE 101), and some programming skills.

Textbooks

You will have access to some chapters of the upcoming textbook:

    P.E.C. Compeau and P.A. Pevzner. Bioinformatics Algorithms. 2014.

Some lectures in the class are covered in:

    N.C. Jones and P.A. Pevzner. Introduction to Bioinformatics Algorithms. The MIT Press. 2004.

For those who have not taken biology we also recommend:

    Larry Gonick and Mark Wheelis. The cartoon guide to genetics. Harperperennial Library. 1991.
