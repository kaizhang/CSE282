[CSE 282/BENG 202] An Introduction to Bioinformatics Algorithms

Instructors: Dr. Terry Gaasterland (SIO) and Dr. Pavel Pevzner (CSE)

Prerequisites

The course assumes some prior background in biology, some algorithmic culture (an equiva-lent of CSE 101), and some programming skills.

Textbooks

There is no required book for this course.

Some lectures in the class are covered in:

N.C. Jones and P.A. Pevzner. Introduction to Bioinformatics Algorithms. The MIT Press. 2004

For those who have not taken biology we also recommend:

Larry Gonick and Mark Wheelis. The cartoon guide to genetics. Harperperennial Library, 1991
